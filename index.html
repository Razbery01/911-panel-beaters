ABOUT 911 PANEL BEATERS

Three Decades of
Precision Repairs

Based in the heart of Jacobs Industrial Area, we've been restoring damaged vehicles to factory condition since the mid-nineties — earning the trust of insurers, fleet operators and drivers across KZN.

What started as a specialist workshop has grown into one of KwaZulu-Natal's most established high-volume repair centres. Proudly Level 1 B-BBEE and black-owned, our qualified team operates across a 1200m² facility using industry-leading systems like Audatex and Abuntex for transparent, insurance-ready quoting.

Advanced Structural Repairer

Insurance Panel Repairer

Fleet & Commercial Experts
