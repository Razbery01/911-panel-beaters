About 911 Panel Beaters

Three Decades of Trusted Accident Repairs

Based in the heart of Jacobs Industrial Area, Durban South, 911 Panel Beaters has been restoring damaged vehicles to factory condition for over 30 years. What started as a specialist workshop has grown into one of KZN's most established high-volume repair centres.

We're a proudly Level 1 B-BBEE black-owned business with a qualified team of trained technicians working across a 1200m² facility. Our scale and systems — including Audatex and Abuntex for accurate quoting — allow us to handle up to 50 vehicles at any one time without compromising on quality.

From insurance repair work to fleet contracts with SAPS, SANDF and commercial operators, our reputation has been built one repair at a time.
